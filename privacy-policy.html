Welcome to Md. Uzzal Hossen's personal portfolio website! This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you visit this website.

1. Information Collection

This website does not collect personal information from visitors.

  • No Forms or Submissions: The site does not include contact forms, newsletters, or any functionality requiring personal data submission.
  • Third-Party Links: Links to client websites and GitHub projects are provided for demonstration purposes. When you click these links, you are subject to the respective third-party site's privacy policy.

2. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies to monitor your browsing activity.

3. External Links

The Portfolio includes:

  • Links to client websites to showcase past work.
  • GitHub project links to demonstrate JavaScript projects.
  • Links to my Upwork profile for hiring purposes.

Please note that clicking these links will direct you to third-party websites. These sites have their own privacy policies, and I am not responsible for their practices or content.

4. Security

This website is designed to prioritize your safety and security. Since no data collection occurs, there is no personal information stored or shared.

5. Purpose

This website solely serves as a portfolio to showcase my professional work and redirect interested clients to my Upwork profile. No advertisements, user data analytics, or third-party integrations are in place.

6. Changes to this Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to the website. Updates will be clearly posted with the effective date at the top of this page.
